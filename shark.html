BLÅHAJ


BLÅHAJ is a large, soft shark designed to bring comfort, fun, and a sense of companionship. Its cuddly body and gentle, friendly expression make it perfect for hugs, play, or simply keeping close on quiet moments.

It has a measure of about 100 cm in length (with a smaller 55 cm version also available), it is made from 100% polyester with recycled hollow-fibre filling, making it both soft and durable. BLÅHAJ comes with embroidered details and meets strict safety standards.
