DJUNGELSKOG (BEAR PLUSH)


Among IKEA’s plush animals, few have reached the same level of popularity as the DJUNGELSKOG bear. Known for its oversized, huggable design, this plush offers a comforting presence that appeals to both children and adults. Its soft texture and generous size make it ideal for cuddling, lounging, or simply adding a cozy touch to a room.

Measuring about 100 cm in length and weighing just over 1 kg, it is generously sized yet soft and lightweight. Made entirely from polyester, with filling from recycled hollow fibre, it is durable and machine-washable. With embroidered details and a design by Annie Huldén
